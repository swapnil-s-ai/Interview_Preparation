# Project Charter

## Project
Data Scientist → Product Company Interview Preparation

## Duration
16 Weeks

## Primary Goal
Become interview-ready for Senior Data Scientist / AI Engineer / GenAI Engineer roles while working full time.

## Success Metrics
- Python interview ready
- SQL interview ready
- ML & GenAI theory confidence
- Intermediate AI/System Design
- Resume and behavioral readiness
- Weekly assessments >=80%

## Constraints
- Weekdays: 2 hours
- Weekends: 4 hours
- Documentation must never reduce study time.

## Definition of Success
Receive interview calls and perform confidently without relying on AI for syntax or explanations.
